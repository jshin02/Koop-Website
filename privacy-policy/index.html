Information We Collect Automatically

Log and Usage Data

  1. We automatically collect and log certain information stored on your terminal device including device type, operating system specification, network settings, unique device identifier and IP address.
  2. Usage data is collected to learn which features are popular, includes data relating to time you are active, and captures buttons and screens you interact with. This helps to inform and improve our product direction and feature development.
  3. Our analytics providers may by default use IP addresses to determine your general non-specific location. Among other things, this allows geographic sorting and protects us against misuse and nefarious activity.

Information collected from cookies and similar technologies

We may receive information from cookies, which are pieces of data your browser stores and sends back to us when making requests, and similar technologies. We use cookies and similar technologies for storing and honoring your preferences and settings, enabling you to sign in, combating fraud, analyzing how our products perform, and fulfilling other legitimate purposes.

Our Retention of Personal Data

Penciv Inc. retains personal data for as long as necessary to provide the products and fulfill the transactions you have requested, or for other legitimate purposes such as complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. Following account deletion, your personal data will be retained for no longer than 90 days, unless it is fulfilling other legal obligations. We will also anonymize some personal data so it will no longer be associated with you. In this event, we are entitled to retain and use the information freely.

Reasons We Share Personal Data

Much of the information on our service is public. By using our service, you are directing us to share information publicly and freely.

When you submit content to be shared with direct recipients (i.e. sharing user created assets), users of our services (i.e. your friends) will be able to see that content and all related metadata (images, emotional feelings, text and voice notes, etc.) attached.

Additionally, we share your personal data with your consent, or as necessary, to complete any transaction or provide any product or service you have requested or authorized. For example, we share your content with third parties (listed in Sharing with Third Parties) when you tell us to do so, such as when you share an asset (message) with a friend. When you provide payment data to make a purchase, we will share payment data with banks and other entities that process payment transactions or provide other financial services, and for fraud prevention and credit risk reduction. When you permit push notifications, the operating system of that device will process some personal data to provide push notifications. Accordingly, we may send data to an external, third-party notification provider to deliver push notifications. Your device's push notification services are governed by their own service-specific terms and privacy statements.

We do not sell your personal information. Your personal information is "sold" when it is provided with a third party for monetary or other valuable consideration for a purpose that is not a "business purpose". A "sale" does not include when we disclose your personal information at your direction, or when otherwise permitted under the law. We may share personal information in the following ways:

  • With your consent
  • With our service providers listed in Sharing with Third Parties
  • To comply with the law
  • In an emergency we may share information if we believe it's necessary to prevent imminent and serious bodily harm to a person
  • To enforce our policies and rights

  • Subscription infrastructure platforms who facilitate the offering of in-app subscriptions. The service providers receive your Account Information.

Deleting Your Account

You may delete your account information at any time from the user preferences page.
